7 things you wanted to know about Nigel Thornberry but were too afraid to ask:

  1. Who is he?

    Sir Nigel Archibald Thornberry, KBE is the host and creator of the nature docuseries "Nigel Thornberry's Wild World" which he co-produces with his wife Marianne, who films and edits the show.
    Nigel is a humble man who prefers to simply be called "Nigel" even after recieving his knighthood from the late Queen Elizabeth II:

  2. Is he a family man?

    Yes. Nigel is a devoted husband to Marianne and a loving father to Debbie and Eliza. His love and genorosity knows no bounds and there was no question in Nigel's mind to adpot a small feral child when he came across him in the wilderness, turning their family of 4 into a family of 5 when he adpoted Donnie.
    Loving animals is second nature to Nigel and while some may find a chimpanzee an unorthodox pet to have, Nigel considers Darwin the chimp just as much a part of the family as his own children. Despite his dedication to his work he always puts his family first and will go to great lengths to protect them:

  3. How much does he really know about animals?

    Nigel is an expert in his field and was even offered a professorship at the prestigious University of Oxford, however after careful consideration Nigel respectfully declined this offer.
